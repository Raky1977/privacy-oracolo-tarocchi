# Privacy Policy for Oracle AI: Tarot Readings

**Last Updated: March 19, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application "Oracle AI: Tarot Readings" (the "App").

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
* **Local Data**: We may ask for your name and date of birth to personalize your tarot readings. This data is stored ONLY locally on your device and is not uploaded to our private servers.
* **Usage Data**: We collect information through third-party tools to improve the App's performance and provide advertisements.

### 2. Third-Party Services (Service Providers)
To provide the service, we use:
* **Google Gemini API**: Your questions and selected cards are sent to Google Gemini to generate interpretations. No personal identification is linked to these queries.
* **Google AdMob**: Used for displaying advertisements (including Rewarded Videos). AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve personalized ads.
* **Firebase Analytics**: Used to understand app usage and crashes.

### 3. US Privacy Compliance (CCPA/COPPA)
* **Children's Privacy (COPPA)**: The App is NOT directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
* **California Privacy (CCPA)**: We do not sell your personal information. Users in California have the right to request data deletion at any time.

### 4. European Union Compliance (GDPR)
For users in the EU, the legal basis for processing data is your consent. You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data by clearing the App's storage or contacting us.

### 5. Data Security
We use industry-standard encryption provided by Google services to protect your data during transit.
